About Himalaya Bhawan

Himalaya Bhawan is one of the newest girls hostels of IIT Roorkee, designed to offer a modern, comfortable, and inclusive living environment for students. Inspired by the strength and resilience of the Himalayas, the Bhawan reflects a blend of contemporary infrastructure and community-oriented spaces.

Established in 2023, Himalaya Bhawan houses students across multiple floors and is equipped with essential facilities including a dining mess, canteen, guest rooms, common recreational areas, laundry facilities, and high-speed wired internet connectivity. With lift access and eco-friendly design elements, the Bhawan focuses on convenience, sustainability, and student well-being.

Facilities

Mess

Himalaya Bhawan Mess provides residents with hygienic and well-balanced meals in a comfortable dining environment. The mess is operated by an authorized catering contractor of IIT Roorkee to ensure consistent quality. The weekly menu is planned under the supervision of the mess warden in coordination with the mess secretary. Separate vegetarian and non-vegetarian dining arrangements ensure an organized dining experience.

Learn more
Service: Daily
Type: Vegetarian & Non-Vegetarian

Canteen

Himalaya Bhawan canteen serves as a convenient food facility for residents throughout the day. It offers a wide variety of food items and packed snacks at reasonable prices. The canteen provides a comfortable and lively environment for students to relax. Located within the Bhawan premises, it is easily accessible to residents.

Study Room

Himalaya Bhawan study room offers a quiet and focused environment for academic activities. The space is designed to support both individual study and group discussions.
